How to Create a MMOWGLI Game Account

Welcome.  We are keen to have you login and play MMOWGLI.  To create a new player account, please select the I'm New to MMOWGLI button.

Sometimes the MMOWGLI game is public, other times access is controlled by invitation.  If a game is by invitation, we will want previously approved email addresses on a VIP list.  If that is not working for you, please send us a Trouble Report.

When you first enter the game page, there are two options. If you are a new user,  please select the left-side text "I'm new to MMOWGLI" to begin the registration process. If you already have an existing account in this game, please select "I'm registered".

You will be asked to read and accept the Terms and Conditions, which are two user agreements. If you agree to these conditions, which protect the privacy of players in the game, then please "accept and continue."  If not then please click "reject, no thanks."

In this area you will be asked to input information that the game will need to create your account. At any point you can close this registration window by left-clicking "Cancel" at right top of the box.  Of course, doing so will abort the process and any registration information you entered will not be saved.

The first box titled "We don't need much to get your playing the game", asks for the email address you want associated with your MMOWGLI. You are also required to create a unique user name which will be used as your identification within the game. After deciding on your game password, please provide it in the fields labeled "Password" and "Confirm Password".

The last required field on this screen is where you are asked to select an "Avatar" image. This will become the image associated with your user name and will show as an icon next to it on most pages of the game. Once you have completed all of the steps above, please select "continue" at the bottom to move to the next screen.

Registration Errors

If you see this error then please re-enter a password of the approriate length. If you are still getting registration errors, please file a Trouble Report.

Tell Us About You

On the "Tell us about you" screen you have the option of telling us about yourself. If you left-click on arrow pointing downward next just right of the Affiliation field, you can select your affiliation. The options here are Non-Profit/NGO, Academic, Military, Government, Industry, Media, or other. After selecting the option that you feel best describes you, you will then be asked to input your location. Once you have entered the information needed above, please left-click "continue".

You will now be on a screen that says "Last Step" at the top of the box. In this screen, you will be asked to input a short description of your pertinent expertise. You will also be asked a counter-piracy trivia question. Upon completion of this please left-click "Get a briefing". This will complete your registration and now you're ready to being game play.

Note: Depending on what stage of development you may or may not be able to join. You will be prompted with this information upon completion of registration and your information will be saved.
