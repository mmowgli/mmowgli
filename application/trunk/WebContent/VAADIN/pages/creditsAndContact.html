Credits

A hard-working team of expert professionals are designing, building and running the MMOWGLI game.

  • Office of Naval Research (ONR): Office of Innovation promotes, fosters, and develops innovative science, technology, processes and policies that support the Department of the Navy. ONR is the project sponsor.
  • Naval Postgraduate School (NPS) provides graduate eduction, research and analysis in modeling and simulation for advanced virtual environments. The Modeling Virtual Environments and Simulation (MOVES) Institute team is building the game, portal, and analytic simulations.
  • Institute For The Future (IFTF) is an independent nonprofit research group that helps organizations of all kinds make better, more informed decisions about the future. IFTF provided the original design for game play and the user user experience.
