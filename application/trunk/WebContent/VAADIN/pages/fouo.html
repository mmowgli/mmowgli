For Official Use Only (FOUO) Guidance

Depending on the needs of the Navy or another government sponsor, some MMOWGLI games can be categorized as For Official Use Only (FOUO).

FOUO is not a classification level.  Rather it denotes that unclassified information does not have unrestricted access.  Formal review of game information by government personnel may be necessary prior to further dissemination.
