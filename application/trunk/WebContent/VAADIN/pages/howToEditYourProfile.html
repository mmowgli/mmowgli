Edit Your Profile

On the top left of the navigation bar next to the logout button, you will see your username.

If you click on that it will bring you over to the "User Profile" screen.

Here is where you can update the Answers to the Game Question and your Expertise. Any award you receive are also shown here.

You can reach this screen for other players, and learn more about them, by clicking on their game names wherever they appear.

Send Private Email

There a link on the "Send a private email" from the profile of the user you wish to send a message.

First update the email Subject line, and then type in your email message under the Content section. When you're done hit the Send button, otherwise you can Cancel.

Just like you, each player has an option to receive in-game email, and another option to receive email to their regular account.  Players can also choose to receive email both ways, or not at all.

Safety check:  email sent to other players does not reveal your actual identity, only your game name.
